Once there was a dog who got lost in the city trying to find a job in barkeology. This little trip he took turned into a real cat-tastrophe when, on one rainy day, the dog was on his way to an interview when a passing car hit a puddle along side the road. The splash drenched the dog, and he barked out at the driver. "What do you think I am a water spaniel". The dog walked into a store to use the bathroom, and used a fan to dry off. So, like this story, every tail has to come to an end.
