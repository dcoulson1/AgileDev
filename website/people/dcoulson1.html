Lost Dog Day...um...Afternoon

One day Wendell, a mutt retriever of dubious patronage, lost his way and found himself lost in a vast metropolis. He paced up and down the vast concrete paths trying to recognize anything. Wendell came upon a large and very old cathedral. He marvelled at the gothic architecture, momentarily forgetting his predicament. He contemplated the workers who built the monument, even the very passage of time. He then relieved himself near the entrance of the church, which raised the ire of a passing constable, a feverish chase ensued. Wendell ran, directionless, his tongue waving like a streamer. Until he was flagged down by a terrier, who beckoned him to salvation inside of an exposed drain pipe. The terrier, diminutive but seemingly authoritative led Wendell to safety undeground. There they hatched a plan.....
