Dieter Frank

Once upon a time, my dog belonged to a different family.

The first family owned a hobby farm, where my dog was eating goats and chickens.

They threw up an ad on craigslist when the father was too lazy to properly discipline and train their dog.

My fiance and I had a pet ferret, and time to train the dog.

Now our dog is reflexively submissive to our ferrets.

Caesar Millan has great simple tips for training dogs.

Our dog ocassionally catches rabbits and pigeons in the yard, but knows and respects the boundaries between our pack and wild animals.
